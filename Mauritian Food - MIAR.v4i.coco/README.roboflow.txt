
Mauritian Food - MIAR - v4 2021-10-30 10:05am
==============================

This dataset was exported via roboflow.ai on October 30, 2021 at 6:05 AM GMT

It includes 1535 images.
Mauritian-food-dataset are annotated in COCO format.

The following pre-processing was applied to each image:
* Auto-orientation of pixel data (with EXIF-orientation stripping)
* Resize to 416x416 (Stretch)
* Auto-contrast via histogram equalization

* 50% probability of horizontal flip
* Random rotation of between -15 and +15 degrees
